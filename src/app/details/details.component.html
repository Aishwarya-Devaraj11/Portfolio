GENERATION OF ELECTRICITY FROM NOISE

ABSTRACT

------>In today’s world, we cannot imagine only one day without using electrical or electronic gadgets.The rapid growth of urbanization and industrialization has led to an increase in ambient noise levels. This project proposes an innovative approach to harness this noise pollution and convert it into a useful form of energy - electricity. The project aims to design a system that captures ambient noise, converts it into electrical energy, and utilizes blockchain technology for efficient energy management.

[1].The first part of the project focuses on the design and development of a noise-to-electricity converter. This device captures ambient noise and converts it into electrical energy through a process known as piezoelectricity. The generated electricity is then stored in a battery for later use.

[2].The second part of the project involves the application of blockchain technology for energy management. Each noise-to-electricity converter is considered a node in the blockchain network. The energy generated by each node is recorded and managed using blockchain, ensuring transparency, security, and efficiency in energy distribution.

EXISTING SYSTEM

[1]:WIND ENERGY

Wind power now represents a major and growing source of renewable energy. Large wind turbines (with capacities of up to 6-8 MW) are widely installed in power distribution networks. Increasing numbers of onshore and offshore wind farms, acting as power plants, are connected directly to power transmission networks at the scale of hundreds of megawatts

[2]:SOLAR ENERGY

The existing system is to consume the maximum solar energy through solar panel. A Solar Tracker is a device onto which solar panels are built-in which tracks the motion of the sun ensuring that maximum amount of sunlight strikes the panels all over the day. Solar tracking allows more energy to be produced because the solar array is able to remain aligned to the sun.

[3]:GENERATION OF ELECTRICITY FROM NOISE

Existing systems focus on converting ambient noise into electrical energy. This is typically achieved through a process known as piezoelectricity, where mechanical strain (caused by sound waves) on certain materials generates an electric charge. The generated electricity can then be stored in a battery for later use.

PROPOSED SYSTEM

[1]:Noise-to-Electricity Converters

There are multiple sources of sound which go unnoticed, most of them is the noise generated by industries, public area, etc. The use of transducers to convert sound waves (noise pollution) into energy demonstrates that noise can act as an alternative source of energy. Similar to existing systems, ambient noise will be converted into electrical energy. However, each converter will also be equipped with the necessary technology to connect to a blockchain network.

[2]:Blockchain Network

Each noise-to-electricity converter will be considered a node in the blockchain network. The energy generated by each node will be recorded on the blockchain, ensuring transparency, security, and efficiency in energy distribution.
