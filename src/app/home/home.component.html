In this project we are going to convert the sound / noise to usable Electrical Energy.The convergence of Blockchain and Internet of Things (IoT) technologies has opened new avenues for innovative solutions in various domains. This project explores the utilization of ambient noise as a potential source for electricity generation, facilitated by Blockchain-IoT integration. The primary objective of this project is to design and implement a system capable of capturing ambient noise and converting it into electrical energy through similar mechanisms. The Blockchain-IoT framework is employed to monitor, manage, and incentivize the electricity generation process efficiently and securely.
